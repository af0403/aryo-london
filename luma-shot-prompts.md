## Global rules for every generation

- Keep the background minimal, pale stone, soft beige, or warm off-white.
- Keep the mood luxury, quiet, sharp, expensive, and fashion-house.
- Avoid loud styling, clutter, props, heavy jewelry, or random streetwear details.
- Do not change the garment construction.
- Keep the noir fabric speckled black and white.
- Keep the silver zipper and snap hardware visible where relevant.
- Keep the trouser fit relaxed and straight, not skinny.
- Keep branding subtle.
- Avoid obvious AI hands, warped seams, or extra pockets.
