## Global visual direction

Every asset should feel:
- luxury
- minimal
- quiet
- architectural
- expensive
- modern but not techy
- soft beige / stone / off-white environments
- no busy interiors
- no fake streetwear energy
- closer to fashion campaign than ecommerce

Avoid:
- cluttered rooms
- obvious AI faces or hands
- too much jewelry
- loud branding
- fake exaggerated tailoring
- random pockets or changed garment construction
- incorrect zipper placement
- incorrect trouser cut

---

## Luma settings

Use these settings as the default:

### For images
- Model: newest high-quality image model available in your Luma UI
- Aspect ratio: use the ratios listed above
- Output: highest quality available
- Format: `png` if available for cleaner reuse, otherwise `jpg`
- Guidance: use references heavily, especially for garment accuracy

### For videos
- Model: newest Ray model available in your UI
- Resolution: `1080p`
- Duration: `8 seconds` by default
- Motion: subtle and controlled
- Camera: slow dolly, slow push, slight pan, or gentle parallax
- Loop: `on` for background / texture / website use

---

## Luma workflow

### Best workflow for the site
1. Generate still images first.
2. Pick the best stills for noir hero, noir jacket, noir trouser, ivory trouser, and ivory jacket concept.
3. Use those stills as start frames for video generation.
4. Keep motion subtle so the site stays premium.

### Why
This gives better consistency than trying to generate video first and then pulling frames from it.
